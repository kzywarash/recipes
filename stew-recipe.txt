Ingrediens:
-Tomato
-Onion
-Groundnut oil
-Ginga and garlic
-Maggi and salt
-Green spices
-Meat(optional)

Directions:
1. Heat oil in pot and fry the sliced tomatoes until ready.
2. Add onions to the fried tomatoes.
3. Add boiled meat to the above mixture and allow to cook for some minutes.
4. Add maggi and salt for taste.
5. Add ginga and garlic and cook for few minutes and turn off the heat.
